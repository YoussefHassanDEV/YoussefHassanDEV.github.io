What is HubSpot?

HubSpot gives agencies a full CRM plus marketing, sales, and service hubs, with a free tier to start and a partner program built for agencies managing client accounts.

👍 Pros

  • Generous free CRM
  • Agency partner program
  • All-in-one platform

👎 Cons

  • Paid tiers get pricey
  • Contact-based pricing scales fast
