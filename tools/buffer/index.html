What is Buffer?

Buffer lets agencies schedule and manage social posts across many client accounts from one clean dashboard, with an AI assistant for captions and ideas.

👍 Pros

  • Clean, simple UI
  • Affordable per-channel pricing
  • AI caption assistant

👎 Cons

  • Fewer analytics than rivals
  • Approval workflows limited on low tiers
