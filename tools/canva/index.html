What is Canva?

Canva lets agencies produce on-brand graphics, decks, and social creative fast, with Brand Kits per client and AI Magic tools for backgrounds, copy, and images.

👍 Pros

  • Per-client Brand Kits
  • Huge template library
  • AI Magic Studio built in

👎 Cons

  • Not a Photoshop replacement
  • Common look if templates unedited
